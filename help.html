F.A.Q

1. Whats the purpose of the website?
- The purpose of E-Fit is to provide you with the best and efficient workouts. Chose your workouts from the workout tabs. You can view how to perform each task in the exercises tab as well as get informed about each muscle

2. Are the exercises effective?
- All exercises included in E-Fit are picked through scientific evaluation

3. Are the workouts e.t.a's correct?
- Yes. Each workout should take you around what the eta says

4. Can I take the workout at my own pace?
- Yes. Although we suggest you try to execute each workout around the given ETA for better efficiency

5. "This feature is not working"?
- We are sorry to hear that. Please contact us using the contacts tab
